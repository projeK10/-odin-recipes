Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

This delicious, tasty, scrambled egg recipe will have you wanting eggs every day! This recipe can be ready in 3 minutes and can serve 3-4 people. This recipe uses US units for the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 8 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 dash of tabasco sauce
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the pan until it starts to bubble.
  2. Pour the eggs in.
  3. Should take 2-3 minutes to cook
  4. Enjoy hot/warm
