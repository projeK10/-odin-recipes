Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe is delicious, and if you like pizza, you will definitely enjoy it! This recipe takes 45 minutes to prepare. It will feed up to 4 people, and the ingredients will be measured in US units.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast
  • cup water (120*)
  • tablespoon oil

Toppings

  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the first 4 ingredients.
  2. Mix water and oil, then add flour to the mixture.
  3. Turn onto floured surface, then knead for 2 minutes.
  4. Place in a greased bowl and turn to grease the top.
  5. Cover, and set aside for 20 minutes.
  6. Punch down the dough and place it on a 12 inch greased pizza pan
  7. Pat into a circle.
  8. Mix the first 5 ingredients and spread over the crust.
  9. Put a few pepperoni slices on top of the sauce
  10. Sprinkle with 1/2 the mozzeralla; 1/2 the parmesan, and 1/2 the monterey jack.
  11. Put the rest of the pepperoni on.
  12. Repeat the cheese layer.
  13. Bake at 400* for 20 minutes, or until it is light brown
