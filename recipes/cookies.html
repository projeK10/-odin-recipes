Chocolate Chip Recipe

Description

This recipe is tasty and will have you wanting more. This recipe will take you about 25 minutes and can serve up to 36 people, depending on how many cookies you make. This recipe uses US units to measure ingredients.

Ingreidents

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of real butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups of firmly packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 12 ounce package of chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt together in a small bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat butter and shortening until creamy.
  3. Add sugar and vanilla and beat with a mixer on medium speed until well blended.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well.
  5. Add flour mixture, and beat slowly to incorporate, then beat to blend well.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  7. Drop batter in 2-tablespoon portions about 2 inches apart onto baking sheets
  8. Bake in a 375*F preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until edges of cookies are brown, but an area of about 1 inch wide in the center is still pale.
  9. If using 2 pans in 1 oven, switch positions at half-time.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool for 5 minutes on the pan before transferring them to the racks with a spatula.
  11. Serve either warm or cool.
